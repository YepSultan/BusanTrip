Subway from Sasang (Line 2)
Direct to Haeundae (14 stops, ~30min) and Gwangan (11 stops). 2-day pass ₩9,000/person. Use T-money card ₩1,500/ride.
🚌
City Bus (a few spots)
Gamcheon: Bus 1-1 from Toseong-dong. Taejongdae: Bus 88 from Nampo-dong. ₩1,500/ride with T-money.
🚕
Kakao Taxi (nights)
Use Kakao T app. Great after midnight. Base ₩4,800. Very affordable split between two.
🏨
Stay near Gwangalli (romantic 💑)
Both Haeundae & Gwangalli on Line 2 — easy access everywhere. Gwangalli = more romantic view of the Diamond Bridge.

🗓️ Itinerary

Apr 24 · Fri Arrive & Haeundae
Apr 25 · Sat ☀️ Gamcheon & Markets
Apr 26 · Sun Cliffs & Head Home
~08:00
🚌 Bus from Jeonju → Busan Sasang
Depart early to maximize the day. ~2h 20min. Buy snacks at the terminal — good ride together.
~10:30
🚇 Sasang → Haeundae (Line 2, ~30min)
Drop bags at hotel. If early check-in not allowed, use coin locker at Haeundae station (₩3,000).
Morning
🏖️ Haeundae Beach Walk
Stroll the 1.5km beach. April = not crowded, perfect weather. Walk to the east end for Dongbaekseom Island.
💑 First photos spot
Lunch
🍜 Milmyeon — Busan's Signature Noodle
Cold wheat noodles born in Busan. The streets near Haeundae have great spots. Very cheap and delicious.
Afternoon
🌿 Dongbaekseom Island Loop
Small forested island connected by a causeway. Camellia trees, quiet ocean paths, very peaceful. 30 min walk.
💑 Most romantic walk in Busan
Sunset
🌅 Gwangalli Beach — Golden Hour
2 stops from Haeundae on Line 2. Diamond Bridge lights up at ~8pm. Sit on the beach and watch together.
💑 Most romantic view in Busan
Night
🍻 Gwangalli Rooftop Dinner + Drinks
Rooftop bars facing the bridge. Budget ~₩30,000–50,000 for two for dinner + drinks.
Morning
🎨 Gamcheon Culture Village
Busan's colourful hillside "Santorini". Tiny alleys, murals, art shops. Go before 11am to avoid crowds. Buy the ₩2,000 stamp map.
💑 Best photo spots in all of Busan
Lunch
🐟 Jagalchi Fish Market
Pick a live fish → pay ₩5,000 extra → eat it cooked upstairs. Full seafood feast for two ~₩30,000–50,000.
Afternoon
🎬 BIFF Square + Gukje Market
Busan Film Festival's famous square. Try the legendary seed hotteok (씨앗호떡) at ₩1,500. Huge traditional market next door.
Late Afternoon
⛩️ Haedong Yonggungsa Temple
Sea-cliff Buddhist temple. Dragon statues, crashing waves. Free entry. Bus 181 from Haeundae (~30min).
💑 Ring the bell together for good luck 🔔
Night
🌃 Gwangalli Night — Best of the Trip
Saturday night bridge light show is spectacular. Walk the beach, rooftop bar, street food. Best night of the trip.
💑 Don't miss this night
Morning
🏔️ Taejongdae Park
Dramatic sea cliffs at Busan's southern tip. Danubi mini-train ₩3,000/person. Lighthouse + ocean views. ~2 hours total.
💑 Hold hands at the cliff edge 🌊
Lunch
🥞 Haemul Pajeon — Seafood Pancake
Crispy seafood & green onion pancake. Busan-style is loaded with squid, shrimp, oysters. Perfect last meal. Best near Nampo-dong.
~15:00
🚇 Head to Sasang Terminal
Subway Line 2 back to Sasang. Check your pre-booked return bus. Buy snacks at GS25 for the ride.
~17:00
🚌 Bus back to Jeonju (~2h 20min)
Time to look back through your photos together 📸 See you next trip!

🍽️ Must-Eat in Busan

🐠
Hoe (Raw Fish)
회 · Jagalchi Market
Pick a live fish from the tank → eat it 10 min later upstairs. Freshest sashimi ever.
💑 Do this together — super fun experience
₩30,000–50,000 for two
🥞
Haemul Pajeon
해물파전 · Seafood pancake
Crispy pancake loaded with squid, shrimp & oysters. Served with soy dipping sauce. Perfect with makgeolli rice wine.
₩12,000–15,000 each
🍜
Milmyeon
밀면 · Unique to Busan
Cold wheat noodles invented in Busan by Korean War refugees. You can't get the real thing anywhere else.
₩8,000–10,000 each
🥐
BIFF Hotteok
씨앗 호떡 · BIFF Square
Seed-filled sweet pancake. Only at BIFF Square. Crispy outside, gooey inside. Worth the queue.
₩1,500 each
🦑
Grilled Squid
오징어 구이 · Beach stalls
Whole squid grilled on a stick. Get it at Haeundae boardwalk at night. Share one — they're big!
💑 Classic beach date food
₩5,000–8,000
🍢
Eomuk (Fish Cake)
어묵 · Busan-famous
Busan fish cakes are legendary. Eat on a skewer with free broth. Perfect for cold April evenings.
₩500–1,000 / skewer
